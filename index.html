StagePad — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

StagePad (the "App") is a stage companion for managing setlists, lyrics, chords and tuning. This policy explains what data the App handles and how. We designed StagePad to keep your data on your own device.

1. Information we collect

We, the developer, do not operate servers and do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data. The App has no account or login.

  • Your content (songs, lyrics, chords, setlists, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only.
  • Export / import is initiated by you: when you export, a file is created that you choose to share; when you import, a file you select is read. We do not transmit these files anywhere.

2. Microphone

The built-in guitar tuner uses your device microphone to detect the pitch of the string you play. Audio is processed in real time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. The microphone is only active while the tuner is running.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process certain information from your device, which can include:

  • Device and advertising identifiers (for example the iOS Identifier for Advertisers, IDFA, when you allow tracking);
  • Approximate, non-precise usage and diagnostic data;
  • Ad interaction data.

This processing is governed by Google's policies, not ours. See:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from apps that use its services

4. Tracking & your choices (iOS App Tracking Transparency)

On iOS, the App asks for your permission before using the IDFA for tracking. If you decline, ads are shown on a non-personalized basis where applicable. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings.

5. Children

StagePad is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Data retention & deletion

Because your content is stored only on your device, you remain in control of it. You can remove it by using the App's reset option or by deleting the App, which removes all locally stored data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
